Igbo Day Celebration Committee

The Event Planning/Activity is responsible for:

Creating/developing a program plan/guest program

﻿﻿Contacting/liaise with Igbo associations for cultural activities

﻿﻿Creating a performance/activities schedules/ types of food desired

﻿﻿Be stage hands/Manager or MC

The Medical/Safety/ Security:

Advises on issues concerning health and safety of guests and participants

﻿﻿Access to emergency medical services

﻿﻿Access to security to ensure the venue is safe

﻿﻿Able to direct visitors to safety in the event of an emergency

The Communication:

Creating and sharing news and events about Igbo Day with community and media

Creating content for and maintain our social media and other outlet to advertise Igoo Day

Creating flyers, posters

Documenting activities day of event

The Resources/Fundraising:

Create & agree on funding plan inclusive of all estimated expenses of other committees

Determining how to solicit/raise funds

Monitoring expenses in each committee

The Logistics

﻿﻿Locate and Arrange appropriate venue for Igbo Day Celebration

Oversees renting, buying, and distribution of all supplies needed for the Igbe Day

﻿﻿Assess supply needs á makes sure committees have what needed to complete assignments

Decorating/Set-up/ Serving meals or food/Venue clean up

Duties or roles are not mutually exclusive, roles can overlap

﻿The roles of committees can be subject to change as deem necessary
