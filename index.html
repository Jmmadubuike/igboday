Welcome to Igbo Day 2025

THEME:OUR CHILDREN, REAWAKENING OUR CULTURAL HERITAGE - "Maka Umuanyi, na Omenala Anyi"

Join us on June 14, 2025, in North Carolina for a celebration of Igbo culture, heritage, and unity.

Learn More

About the Event

Igbo Day 2025 is an event dedicated to celebrating the rich cultural heritage of the Igbo people. This year's event will be held in North Carolina and will feature a variety of activities that highlight our traditions, art, music, and cuisine. We aim to foster community spirit and cultural pride among the Igbo diaspora.

Event Highlights

Authentic Igbo Cuisine

Networking Opportunities

Igbo Music and Drumming

Traditional Dance Performances

Cultural Exhibitions and Art Displays

Speeches from Community Leaders and Special Guests
