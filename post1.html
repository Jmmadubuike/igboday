North Carolina Igbo Day Festival - June 14th, 2025

Posted on July 4, 2024 by Felicia Chika Harrison

Dear Ndiigbonile, friends, and well-wishers of the Igbos both in North Carolina & beyond, & the Nigerian community,the organizing committee greets you all!

Get Ready! Be Ready!! Stay Ready!!! Plan ahead of time to avoid any potential date conflict...!

The North Carolina Igbo Day Foundation is inviting the general public to this year's Igbo Day Festival Celebration.

DATE: JUNE 14TH, 2025.
TIME: SUNRISE - SUNSET
VENUE: FLOWER FIELD COTTAGE AT DOROTHEA DIX PARK, 2105 UMSTEAD DRIVE, RALEIGH NC 27603.

Join us to see what the organizing committee has prepared to showcase to the public and the Nigerian Community. The event will feature the history of the Igbo people of Nigeria, different traditional Igbo mouthwatering delicacies, cultural dancers, Igbo masquerades, and a music festival.

We are soliciting your support through purchasing brochure ads, t-shirts, sponsorship, etc. It would be a fun-filled event that you will regret missing!

Join us!
We are encouraging everyone to kindly save the date, and plan to attend.

Signed

Chief/Mrs. Felicia Chika Harrison: Communication Committee (Chair)
Lady Lillian Oby Ezuma - Communication Committee (Co-Chair)
